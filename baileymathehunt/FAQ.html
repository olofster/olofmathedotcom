WTF is going on? 

Bailey Mathé & Hunt is a performance (initiated during Art Hack Day Berlin: “Going Dark”) that explores mediated identity, human self-worth and the viability of art. This Tumblr is where the auction, account transfer and subsequent life of the Facebook profiles will be documented. 



When will the account transfer take place? 

The profiles will change hands Wednesday November 13, 2013. RSVP for the transfer here



Why Bailey, Mathé & Hunt?

The partners of the esteemed auction house Bailey Mathé & Hunt have significant expertise in digital trafficking and online personality mediation. They are honored to facilitate the transfer of famous new media artist Facebook accounts to new owners.
